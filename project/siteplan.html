My Site Plan

Site Name: ImportCarMart

This name represents a platform focused on selling imported cars and car parts. The site helps local small businesses showcase their products to potential buyers.

Optional domain availability: importcarmart.com

Site Purpose

The website serves as an online platform for small car dealers to display and sell imported cars and car parts. It offers a space for businesses to connect with customers looking for specific vehicles or parts.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A user wants to know if the website sells a specific car part for their vehicle.
  • Scenario 2: A user is interested in browsing imported cars and checking their prices before making a purchase.

Color Schema

Primary Color: Smoky Black (#312001) - Used for headers, footers, and buttons.

Accent Color: Light Gray (#E0E0E0) - Used for backgrounds and section dividers.

Typography

Headings: Font: Roboto, sans-serif - Applied to all headings.

Body Text: Font: Open Sans, sans-serif - Used for the body text and paragraphs.
